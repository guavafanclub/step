Hobbies

Crocheting

I began crocheting in highschool and have picked it back up in recent months. I've crocheted teddy bears, stuffed elephants, stuffed cats, and a hat. I've been crocheting a blanket for what feels like forever.

Reading

I'm almost 99% sure my eyesight deteriorated because I used to read books by moonlight when I was supposed to be sleeping. As I've grown up, I've had less time to dedicate to reading, but I still try to fit a book in here and there.
My favorite books:

  • A Tale for the Time Being by Ruth Ozeki
  • The Giver by Lois Lowry
  • Gone Girl by Gillian Flynn

Cooking

Despite my mom teaching me how to cook our cultural Liberian dishes since I was a preteen, I've only now begun to find joy in cooking. I like to make random dishes I find on the internet and Liberian dishes I grew up eating.
